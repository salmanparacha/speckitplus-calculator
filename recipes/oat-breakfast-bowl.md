# Fresh Oat Breakfast Bowl

## Ingredients

- 1 cup rolled oats
- 2 cups milk (dairy or plant-based)
- 1 fresh banana, sliced
- 1/2 cup fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, or raspberries - local when possible)
- 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (local preferred)
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp sea salt
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- Fresh mint leaves for garnish

## Instructions

1. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine oats, milk, cinnamon, and salt
2. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until oats are creamy and tender
3. Remove from heat and stir in chia seeds
4. Transfer to a bowl and top with fresh sliced banana and berries
5. Drizzle with honey or maple syrup
6. Garnish with fresh mint leaves
7. Serve warm

## Notes

- For overnight oats: combine all ingredients except toppings in a jar, refrigerate overnight, and add fresh toppings in the morning
- Customize with seasonal local fruits
- Adjust sweetness to taste
- No nuts or tree nuts included - safe for nut allergies
